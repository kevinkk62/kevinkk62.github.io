Not A Good House

"Not A Good House" is a first-person psychological horror game where you investigate the disappearance of several boys many years ago. All clues point to an old, isolated house that's become the central character of the game. You'll explore detailed, claustrophobic environments, solve twisted puzzles, and uncover the chilling truth that the boys were not simply lost but trapped and victimized within the house's walls. The game promises an intense, immersive narrative with twists, a creepy atmosphere, challenging puzzles, and a dark story about hidden evil, all enhanced by a tension-building soundtrack.
